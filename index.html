Iron North

logo + identity + assets

Local Hintonburg gym Iron North doesn’t just have an epic name; it has an epic ownership. It’s co-founder Jenna, now running the operation solo, exudes energy. Besides leading high-paced, high-fun workouts and keeping the operation sailing smoothly, she and her staff know everyone at the gym by name and schedule — she considers them family. The branding we did at Salt reflects that dedication to the community. Bright, bold colours on a largely black brand speak to both her gym’s upbeat energy and badass nature, and a new logo with a northern star atop a mountain of typography, coupled with medieval sigil-inspired assets, crown Iron North as the local bastion of strength, exercise and good times.

Kerden Flax

logo + identity + packaging + phptography + Shopify website

Let’s face it… flax doesn’t have the most fun reputation. Isn’t that what grandma puts in her juice? But flaxseed is actually an amazing, low-cost source of Omega-3s, and in this health-conscious era of smoothies and keto salads, Kerden has the opportunity to give flax the spot in the limelight it deserves. With that in mind, we set off to create a brand that would stand apart in the sometimes uh… unexciting health food space. Inspired by ancient artwork from Egypt — where some of the first flax was harvested — Kerden’s brand and packaging use rich, eye-catching patterns in tones of sand, ochre and clay, and a logo inspired by their god of harvest. Their unique selling point — the only flax in Canada with patented technology that allows them to dehull the flax seeds without damaging them — made coming up with their slogan a cinch. No Husk, No Fuss.

Bluebarn Coffee

logo + identity + assets + packaging

The folks behind Bluebarn wanted to elevate their brand and came to us looking to infuse it with their appreciation for discovery and the finer things in life. At the root of their company culture is the drive to discover the best — the best beans, the best relationships, the best cup. This attitude is reflected not just in their coffee, but in the aesthetics of their roastery barn: old tin jars, nostalgic paraphernalia and hand-painted signage are a part of their identity. Inspired by these visuals, we created a brand that feels as evocative, adventurous and crafted as the materials and ingredients they love and share.

Algonquin College

identity standards + identity application + illustration + art direction

As part of their 2023 Strategic Plan, Algonquin College approached Salt with the task of evolving their collegiate, community-college visual identity with a more sophisticated look and feel that matched their updated academic offerings and international presence. The challenge? Retain the core brand elements: their logo, Gotham typeface, and the iconic Algonquin green. With that, they needed a fresh set of graphic standards that would assist the marketing team in keeping the brand in check. Our solution came in the form of a thorough but flexible, 86-page set of graphic standards that introduced a fresh, tone-based colour palette revolving around the green and an elegant serif typeface to complement the more playful Gotham. A fun and easy-to-follow illustration style gave them new tools to expand their brand while keeping it youthful and lively, and fresh photography benchmarks set a leadership tone. Today, the illustrations, banners, ads, newsletters and other materials we made and/or art directed can be seen across all three of Algonquin’s campuses, and on buses all around town.

OK! Kombucha

logo + identity + packging + collateral

OK! Kombucha had their sights set on bringing a non-alcoholic option for those who want to have a delicious beverage while out — and not just because it’s healthy. Co-founder, Safa, wanted to bring flavour into the equation. Bold, inventive and just straight-up delicious, OK! Kombucha stands out from the crowd all on merit alone. We wanted the packaging to contribute. Using bold, vibrant colours that reference each of the bottles’ flavours, and swooping patterns and shapes that speak of their booch’s fizziness, we created bottles that would be both fun and elegant to hold at any daytime coffee shop or nighttime lounge.
